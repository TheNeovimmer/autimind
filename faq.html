AutiMind is a comprehensive digital platform designed to support children with autism and their families. It offers early screening tools, interactive learning activities, progress tracking, and communication aids — all in one secure environment.

AutiMind is built for children with autism (ages 2–16), their parents and caregivers, as well as educators and healthcare specialists working in autism care and support.

Our platform includes AI-powered early screening, gamified learning activities, behavior tracking tools, communication aids, progress analytics, specialist consultation booking, and a resource library for families.
