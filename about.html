ABOUT US

Empowering autism
care through technology

AutiMind is a comprehensive digital platform designed to support early screening, skill development, and daily communication for children with autism and their families.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to communicate, learn, and thrive. Our platform bridges the gap between families and professional support through evidence-based tools, AI-powered screening, and personalized learning pathways.

Built with input from healthcare professionals, educators, and families, AutiMind puts world-class autism support tools into the hands of those who need them most.

Why AutiMind

Early Screening

AI-powered assessments that help identify developmental patterns and provide actionable insights.

Interactive Learning

Gamified exercises and activities that build communication, social, and cognitive skills.

Family Support

Resources and tools designed to help parents and caregivers navigate the journey with confidence.

Progress Tracking

Detailed reports and analytics to monitor development and share progress with care teams.
